I love animals and my favourite activity is spending time with my cat, Coco, and my dog, Pal. Though, they are not as much of my pets as I am their human.
I enjoy going for long exploratory walks with Pal, who likes to bring his friends along. As soon as we go out, the neighbouring dogs start howling until they are let out to join us so I end up walking the neightbours' dogs as well. It's great fun! Pal also enjoys being my co-pilot when we go for drives. We live in a pretty rural area so he's allowed in the front seat "giving directions".
Coco is less of an activity companion and more of a rest mate. Basically he spends his time asleep near me, or on me. Purring away.
Coco came to my life when he was a tiny baby and he grew up looking after me when I was very unwell and had to be on bed rest for a few months. He's the best nurse I could asked for. Pal just turned up one day at our house and decided he was going to stay, and, quite frankly, who could say no to that face?
We've all lived happily ever since and I can't imagine my life without them. They are such a big part of my life and have enriched it so much that I want people to know how great sharing a life with an animal can be so I am now involved in a local charity organization helping rescue and rehome animals in our town.
